# ICP 1: Solo Entrepreneur / Solopreneur

## Demographics
- **Age**: 28-45
- **Location**: Global (English-speaking markets)
- **Role**: Founder, freelancer, consultant, content creator
- **Company Size**: Self-employed or 1-2 people
- **Income**: $50K-$150K/year
- **Tech Savviness**: Medium to High

## Psychographics
- **Goals**: 
  - Scale business without hiring
  - Automate repetitive tasks
  - Increase productivity and output
  - Stay competitive with larger players
  
- **Values**: 
  - Efficiency
  - Independence
  - Continuous learning
  - Innovation

## Pain Points
1. **Time Scarcity**: Wearing too many hats, drowning in admin work
2. **Limited Budget**: Can't afford to hire a full team
3. **Knowledge Gap**: Knows AI exists but overwhelmed by options
4. **Fear of Complexity**: Worried AI is too technical to implement
5. **FOMO**: Competitors are using AI, feeling left behind

## Buying Triggers
- Seeing case studies with clear ROI
- Free trial or low-risk entry point
- Step-by-step implementation support
- Community of similar users
- Quick wins (results in days, not months)

## Where They Hang Out
- LinkedIn (following thought leaders)
- Twitter/X (tech communities)
- YouTube (searching "how to automate...")
- Reddit (r/Entrepreneur, r/SideProject)
- Indie Hackers
- Product Hunt

## Key Messages That Resonate
- "Get your time back"
- "Scale without hiring"
- "AI that works for you, not against you"
- "From overwhelmed to automated in 30 days"

## Objections
- "Too expensive"
- "Too complicated for someone like me"
- "I don't have time to learn this"
- "What if it doesn't work for my specific business?"

## How to Reach Them
- LinkedIn posts with personal stories
- Short-form video tutorials
- Free tools/templates
- Case studies from similar businesses
- Email sequences with quick wins
