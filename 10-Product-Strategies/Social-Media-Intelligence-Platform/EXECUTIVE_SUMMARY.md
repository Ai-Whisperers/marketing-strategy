## RISKS & MITIGATION

### Risk 1: Customers don't see value from free reports
**Likelihood**: Low (we're giving them $500-1,000 of value)
**Mitigation**: Personalize summaries, highlight specific pain points, offer to walk them through

### Risk 2: Conversion rate lower than expected (20%)
**Likelihood**: Medium (unproven model)
**Mitigation**: A/B test messaging, improve demo script, offer 30-day money-back guarantee

### Risk 3: Multi-company management takes longer to build
**Likelihood**: Medium (8-9 days is estimate)
**Mitigation**: Start now, hire contractor if needed, launch MVP first

### Risk 4: Competition from established players
**Likelihood**: Low (we have LATAM advantage + lower price)
**Mitigation**: Move fast, build case studies, lock in customers with annual contracts

### Risk 5: Platform changes break scrapers
**Likelihood**: Medium (Instagram/Facebook change HTML frequently)
**Mitigation**: Playwright is resilient, build error detection, have fallback to manual upload
