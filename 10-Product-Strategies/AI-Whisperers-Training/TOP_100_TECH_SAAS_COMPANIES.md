## SCORING METHODOLOGY (30 Points Maximum)

### Scoring Criteria (1-5 each, 6 categories)

1. **SIZE (1-5)** - Revenue & market presence
2. **SOCIAL (1-5)** - LinkedIn presence & tech community engagement
3. **DIGITAL NEED (1-5)** - AI transformation urgency
4. **CX VOLUME (1-5)** - Customer-facing operations scale
5. **TRAINING BUDGET (1-5)** - L&D investment capacity
6. **ACCESSIBILITY (1-5)** - Ease of reaching decision-makers

### Service Categories
- **A** = Comment Analyzer (Social Media Intelligence)
- **B** = Training Primary ⭐ (AI Upskilling for workforce)
- **C** = Both (Dual opportunity)

**NOTE:** Tech companies are typically **Category B** - they need AI training for developers, QA engineers, sales teams, customer success, and business professionals.
