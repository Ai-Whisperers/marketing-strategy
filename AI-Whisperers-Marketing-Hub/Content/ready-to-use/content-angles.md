## Content Angle Distribution Strategy

### Weekly Content Mix (Suggested)
- **40%**: Quick Wins & Actionable Tips (high volume, high engagement)
- **25%**: Behind-the-Scenes (builds trust and authenticity)
- **20%**: Education & Skill Building (builds authority)
- **10%**: Data-Driven Results (social proof and credibility)
- **5%**: Future of Work (thought leadership)

### By Funnel Stage

**Top of Funnel (Awareness):**
- Quick Wins (50%)
- Education (30%)
- Behind-the-Scenes (20%)

**Middle of Funnel (Consideration):**
- Data-Driven Results (40%)
- Education (40%)
- Behind-the-Scenes (20%)

**Bottom of Funnel (Decision):**
- Data-Driven Results (60%)
- Behind-the-Scenes (30%)
- Future of Work (10%)
