# Growth Programs

> Scale through partnerships, referrals, and affiliates

---

## Overview

Growth programs leverage other people and businesses to expand your reach. These include:

1. **Referral Program** - Existing clients refer new clients
2. **Partnership Program** - Strategic alliances with complementary businesses
3. **Affiliate Program** - Anyone can earn commission for referrals

## Program Comparison

| Program | Best For | Effort | Potential |
|---------|----------|--------|-----------|
| Referrals | Warm leads from happy clients | Low | High quality |
| Partnerships | Strategic reach expansion | Medium | High volume |
| Affiliates | Scalable passive acquisition | High setup | Variable |

---

## Priority Order

1. **Start with referrals** - Lowest effort, highest quality leads
2. **Build partnerships** - Once you have capacity
3. **Consider affiliates** - When ready to scale

---

## Key Metrics

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Referral rate | 20%+ of new clients from referrals |
| Partner-sourced leads | 10-20% of pipeline |
| Affiliate conversion | 2-5% of affiliate traffic |
