## Design Specifications

### Layout:
- **Container**: Max-width 1200px, centered
- **Three columns**: Equal width on desktop, stack on mobile
- **Project cards**: Grid layout, 3 columns on desktop, 1 on mobile
- **Spacing**: Generous padding between sections (60-80px)

### Colors:
- **Background**: Light gray or white
- **Cards**: White with subtle shadow
- **Tech badges**: Brand color with light background
- **Icons**: Brand color or gradient

### Typography:
- **Section headline**: Large, bold (36-48px)
- **Subheadline**: Medium (18-20px), gray
- **Project titles**: Bold (24-28px)
- **Body text**: Readable (16-18px), dark gray

### Interactive Elements:
- **Hover effect**: Cards lift slightly on hover
- **GitHub buttons**: Outline style with hover fill
- **Links**: Brand color, underline on hover
