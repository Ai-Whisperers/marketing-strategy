## Why Repurpose?

Creating one good video takes hours. That same content can fuel your marketing for weeks across every platform - if you have a system.

**The Math**:
- 1 video = 4 hours of work
- Repurposed content = 1 hour additional
- Total output = 20+ pieces of content
- ROI per hour: 5x improvement

## Step-by-Step Process

### Step 1: During Video Production

While scripting, mark potential clips:
- [ ] Strong opening hook (first 30 seconds)
- [ ] Key insight moments
- [ ] Quotable one-liners
- [ ] Demonstration highlights
- [ ] Summary/recap section

### Step 2: After Publishing (Same Day)

**Immediate repurposing** (30 min):
1. Post on LinkedIn with key insight
2. Share to Twitter with hook
3. Send to email list

### Step 3: Within 48 Hours

**Short-form clips** (1 hour):
1. Extract 3-5 clips (30-60 seconds each)
2. Add captions (required for social)
3. Create vertical versions for Shorts/Reels/TikTok
4. Schedule throughout the week

### Step 4: Within 1 Week

**Written content** (1.5 hours):
1. Write blog post from transcript
2. Create Twitter thread
3. Design carousel from main points
4. Create quote graphics

## Repurposing by Content Type

### YouTube Shorts / TikTok / Reels (Vertical Video)

**What to extract**:
- Best 30-60 second segments
- Hook moments
- Quick tips or reveals
- Behind-the-scenes moments

**Requirements**:
- Vertical format (9:16)
- Captions required
- Strong hook in first 3 seconds
- Loop-worthy if possible

**Tools**: CapCut, OpusClip (AI), Descript

---

### LinkedIn Posts

**Format 1: Insight Post**
```
[Hook line from video]

Here's what most people get wrong:
[Key insight]

What I've learned from helping 50+ businesses:
[2-3 bullet points]

The bottom line:
[Main takeaway]

Full breakdown in my new video: [link]
```

**Format 2: Story Post**
```
[Relatable problem or story opener]

This happened to a client recently...

[Brief story from video]

The lesson:
[Takeaway]

I break down exactly how we solved it here: [link]
```

---

### Twitter/X Thread

**Structure**:
```
Tweet 1: [Hook - the boldest claim or question]

Tweet 2: Here's what most people don't realize...
[First key point]

Tweet 3: [Second key point with data/example]

Tweet 4: [Third key point]

Tweet 5: [Fourth key point]

Tweet 6: The bottom line:
[Summary]

Full video breakdown here: [link]
```

---

### Blog Post

**From video to blog process**:
1. Get transcript (YouTube auto-generates, or use Descript)
2. Clean up transcript (remove filler, ums, repetition)
3. Add headers for each section
4. Add intro paragraph
5. Add images/screenshots from video
6. Add internal links
7. Optimize for SEO (different from video SEO)
8. Add CTA at end

**Time**: 30-45 minutes with transcript

---

### Email Newsletter

**Template**:
```
Subject: [Key insight from video]

Hey [Name],

Quick insight this week:

[2-3 paragraph summary of video]

[Key takeaway or action item]

Watch the full breakdown: [link]

[Your name]

P.S. [Additional hook or upcoming content tease]
```

---

### Carousel (LinkedIn/Instagram)

**Structure** (8-10 slides):
1. Hook/title slide
2. Problem statement
3. Point 1
4. Point 2
5. Point 3
6. Point 4
7. Summary/key takeaway
8. CTA slide

**Design tips**:
- Consistent branding
- One idea per slide
- Large, readable text
- Swipe indicator on first slide

---

### Quote Graphics

**Best quotes from videos**:
- Surprising statistics
- Bold claims
- Memorable one-liners
- Actionable advice

**Design**:
- Clean background
- Large quote text
- Your photo or logo
- Branded colors

---

### Audiogram

**What it is**: Audio clip with waveform visualization

**Best for**:
- Twitter/X
- LinkedIn
- Podcast promotion

**Tools**: Headliner, Wavve, Canva

**Best clips**:
- Emotional moments
- Surprising insights
- Controversial takes

## Content Multiplier Example

**Repurposed Into**:

2. Short: Task #2 quick tip (45 sec)

5. LinkedIn post: "The 5 tasks stealing your time"
6. LinkedIn carousel: "5 automations in 5 slides"
7. Twitter thread: "5 automations that save 10+ hrs/week"
8. Blog post: "5 Tasks Every Business Should Automate"
9. Email: "Your 5 quick wins this week"
10. Quote graphic: "Setup time: 10 min. Time saved: 2-4 hrs/week"
11. Quote graphic: "Chasing payments wastes time AND affects cash flow"
12. Audiogram: The section on follow-ups

**Total**: 12+ pieces from 1 video
