# Video Strategy

> Video builds trust faster than any other medium

---

## Why Video?

- YouTube is the #2 search engine
- Video content gets 1200% more shares than text and images combined
- 90% of customers say video helps them make buying decisions
- Video humanizes your brand and builds trust fast

---

## Our Video Strategy

### Channel Focus: YouTube

**Why YouTube**:
- Evergreen content (videos rank for years)
- Search-driven discovery
- Establishes authority
- Repurposable to all other platforms

**Channel Goal**:
Become the go-to resource for practical AI automation for business

### Content Pillars

| Pillar | % of Content | Examples |
|--------|--------------|----------|
| **How-To Tutorials** | 40% | "How to automate [task] in 10 minutes" |
| **Results/Case Studies** | 25% | "How [client] saved 15 hours/week" |
| **Tool Reviews** | 20% | "ChatGPT vs Claude for business" |
| **Thought Leadership** | 15% | "The future of AI automation" |

## Video Types

### Tutorial Videos (8-15 min)
**Purpose**: Teach a specific skill
**Format**: Screen share + talking head
**Example**: "How to Create AI-Powered Email Templates in 10 Minutes"

### Case Study Videos (5-10 min)
**Purpose**: Show real results
**Format**: Talking head + graphics/data
**Example**: "How We Helped a Law Firm Save 20 Hours Per Week"

### Comparison Videos (10-15 min)
**Purpose**: Help with decisions
**Format**: Screen share + talking head
**Example**: "Zapier vs Make vs n8n - Which Automation Tool is Best?"

### Quick Tips (2-5 min)
**Purpose**: Quick value, high shareability
**Format**: Talking head or screen share
**Example**: "The One AI Prompt That Saves Me 2 Hours Daily"

## Publishing Cadence

### Phase 1: Foundation (Month 1-2)
- 1 video per week
- Focus on evergreen how-to content
- Build library of 8-10 videos

### Phase 2: Growth (Month 3-6)
- 2 videos per week
- Mix of tutorials and case studies
- Start optimizing based on data

### Phase 3: Scale (Month 6+)
- 2-3 videos per week
- Add shorts/clips
- Guest appearances, collaborations

---

## Video Production Process

### Pre-Production (Day 1)
- [ ] Choose topic from content calendar
- [ ] Research keywords and competition
- [ ] Write script outline
- [ ] Create thumbnail concept

### Production (Day 2)
- [ ] Set up equipment
- [ ] Record talking head sections
- [ ] Record screen share sections
- [ ] Get B-roll if needed

### Post-Production (Day 3)
- [ ] Edit video
- [ ] Add graphics, text, music
- [ ] Create thumbnail
- [ ] Write title, description, tags

### Publishing (Day 4)
- [ ] Upload to YouTube
- [ ] Schedule or publish
- [ ] Create clips for social media
- [ ] Promote on LinkedIn, email
