## Shorts/Clips Strategy

### Extract from Long-Form Videos
Each long-form video should yield 2-3 Shorts:
- Key insight (30-60 sec)
- Quick tip (15-30 sec)
- Surprising stat/fact (15-30 sec)

### Standalone Shorts Ideas
- [ ] "One prompt that saves me 2 hours daily"
- [ ] "The automation everyone forgets"
- [ ] "ChatGPT trick nobody talks about"
- [ ] "Why I stopped using [tool]"
- [ ] "3 seconds to automate this"

### Shorts Schedule
- Post 3-5 Shorts per week
- Best times: Early morning, lunch, evening
- Use trending sounds when relevant
