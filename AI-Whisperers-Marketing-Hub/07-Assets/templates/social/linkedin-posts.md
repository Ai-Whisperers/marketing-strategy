## Formatting Best Practices

### Hook (First 2 Lines)
The hook appears above "...see more" - make it count:
- Bold statement
- Intriguing question
- Surprising statistic
- Pattern interrupt

### Structure
- Short paragraphs (1-3 lines)
- White space between sections
- Bullet points for lists
- Emojis sparingly (1-3 per post)

### Ending
- Clear call to action
- Engagement prompt (question)
- Save/share request

## Engagement Tactics

### Before posting:
- Engage on 5-10 posts for 15-20 minutes
- Warm up the algorithm

### After posting:
- Respond to every comment within first hour
- Ask follow-up questions
- Tag relevant people (with permission)

### To boost reach:
- Edit post within first hour (triggers re-distribution)
- Save posts to trigger algorithm
- DM people who engage to continue conversation
