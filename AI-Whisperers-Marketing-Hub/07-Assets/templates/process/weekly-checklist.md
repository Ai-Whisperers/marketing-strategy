# Weekly Marketing Checklist

> A repeatable routine for consistent marketing execution

---

## Monday: Plan & Prepare

### Morning (30 min)
- [ ] Review last week's metrics (quick scan)
- [ ] Check this week's content calendar
- [ ] Identify any gaps to fill
- [ ] Set 3 priorities for the week
- [ ] **Set daily outreach target: minimum 20 touches**

### Afternoon (30 min)
- [ ] Engage on LinkedIn (15 min)
- [ ] Respond to messages/comments from weekend
- [ ] Schedule any meetings needed
- [ ] Prepare materials for tomorrow's content

---

## Tuesday: Create & Publish

### Morning (60 min)
- [ ] Create primary content piece (post, article, video)
- [ ] Design any needed graphics
- [ ] Write supporting copy
- [ ] Schedule or publish content

### Afternoon (30 min)
- [ ] Engage on LinkedIn (15 min)
- [ ] Send any cold outreach emails
- [ ] Follow up on warm conversations
- [ ] Respond to post engagement

---

## Wednesday: Outreach & Connect

### Morning (60 min)
- [ ] Send LinkedIn connection requests (10-20)
- [ ] Send personalized outreach messages (5-10)
- [ ] Follow up on pending conversations
- [ ] Update CRM with new interactions

### Afternoon (30 min)
- [ ] Engage on LinkedIn (15 min)
- [ ] Check email campaign performance
- [ ] Adjust any underperforming campaigns
- [ ] Prepare Thursday's content

---

## Thursday: Content & Engage

### Morning (60 min)
- [ ] Create second content piece for the week
- [ ] Repurpose existing content (clips, quotes)
- [ ] Comment thoughtfully on 10-15 posts
- [ ] Share/engage with partner content

### Afternoon (30 min)
- [ ] Engage on LinkedIn (15 min)
- [ ] Respond to all comments on your content
- [ ] Send any needed follow-ups
- [ ] Check lead pipeline status

---

## Friday: Review & Optimize

### Morning (60 min)
- [ ] Complete weekly KPI dashboard
- [ ] Analyze content performance
- [ ] Identify top performer - why did it work?
- [ ] Identify underperformer - what can we learn?

### Afternoon (30 min)
- [ ] Plan next week's content
- [ ] Update content calendar
- [ ] Clear inbox/messages
- [ ] Set Monday priorities

---

## Daily Habits (15-20 min/day)

### Every Day
- [ ] Check and respond to LinkedIn messages
- [ ] Engage with 5-10 posts (meaningful comments)
- [ ] Check email for lead inquiries
- [ ] Update CRM with any new interactions
- [ ] **Complete minimum 20 outreach touches**

### Time Blocks
- **Morning**: Content creation, strategic work
- **Afternoon**: Engagement, admin, outreach
- **End of day**: Clear inbox, prep for tomorrow

---

## Weekly Minimums

| Activity | Weekly Target |
|----------|---------------|
| LinkedIn posts | 3-5 |
| LinkedIn engagement (comments) | 50+ |
| Connection requests | **50 minimum** |
| Outreach messages | **100/week (20/day)** |
| Follow-ups | 15-20 |
| Content pieces created | 2-3 |
| Discovery calls | 3+ |

---

## Monthly Additions

### Week 1
- [ ] Review and update ICP documentation
- [ ] Analyze lead source performance
- [ ] Refresh any stale outreach sequences

### Week 2
- [ ] Create one long-form content piece
- [ ] Update case studies if new data available
- [ ] Review competitive landscape

### Week 3
- [ ] A/B test review and learnings
- [ ] Partner/referral outreach
- [ ] Content repurposing sprint

### Week 4
- [ ] Monthly performance review
- [ ] Strategy adjustment based on data
- [ ] Plan next month's priorities
- [ ] Celebrate wins!
