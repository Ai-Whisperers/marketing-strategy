## 3. PAIN POINT DIAGNOSIS

### Primary Pain Points for Target Vets

1. **No Online Presence**
   - Most small vets have no website or outdated Facebook page
   - Losing clients to chains with professional online presence
   - Cannot capture after-hours appointment requests

2. **Manual Operations**
   - Paper records = lost files, inefficiency
   - Phone-only scheduling = missed calls, lost revenue
   - No automated reminders = missed appointments

3. **Competition from Chains**
   - Large veterinary chains dominate search results
   - Professional branding vs. "local vet" perception

4. **Cost Barriers**
   - Traditional website = Gs. 5-10 million upfront
   - Monthly hosting/maintenance = additional costs
   - Most small vets cannot justify the investment

### Urgency Triggers
- Growing pet ownership in Paraguay post-pandemic
- Increasing smartphone/internet penetration
- Younger pet owners expect digital convenience
