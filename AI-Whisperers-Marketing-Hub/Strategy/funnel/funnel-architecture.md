### Goal

### The Buying Committee Reality
| Stakeholders | % of Purchases | Implication |
|--------------|----------------|-------------|
| 1-4 people | 18% | Fast decision possible |
| 4-8 people | 31% | Create shareable content |
| 8-12 people | 29% | Help champion sell internally |
| 12+ people | 22% | Multiple touchpoints needed |

### Sales Process

#### Discovery Call (30-45 min)
**Structure**:
1. Build rapport (5 min)
2. Understand current state (10 min)
3. Identify pain points (10 min)
4. Present vision of future state (10 min)
5. Introduce solution (5 min)
6. Next steps (5 min)

**Goals**:
- Qualify fit
- Understand budget
- Establish urgency
- Book follow-up or close

#### Proposal/Pitch (for $5K+ deals)

**Winning Structure** (research shows these convert best):
| Section | % of Proposal | Purpose |
|---------|---------------|---------|
| Executive Summary | 10% | High-level pain + solution |
| Problem Statement | 15% | Show you understand them |
| Proposed Solution | 30% | How you'll solve it |
| Proof | 15% | Case studies, testimonials |
| Investment & Timeline | 15% | Transparent, ROI-focused |
| Next Steps | 10% | Clear CTA |

**Delivery**:
- Loom video walkthrough
- PDF document
- Live presentation (optional)
- Follow-up within 48 hours

#### Closing Techniques (Psychology-Based)

| Barrier | Technique | Script |
|---------|-----------|--------|
| Fear of wrong decision | Risk reversal | "It will work. And it won't take forever." |
| Need internal approval | Help champion | "I'll prepare a one-pager for [decision maker]" |
| Budget concerns | ROI focus | "Your current cost is $X/year. Investment is 1/5 of that." |
| Analysis paralysis | Recommend one | "Based on your situation, I recommend [option]" |

### Follow-Up Cadence (80% of sales need 5+ follow-ups)

```
Day 0:  PROPOSAL SENT
Day 2:  Check-in ("Had a chance to review?")
Day 5:  Value-add (share relevant case study)
Day 7:  Phone call attempt
Day 10: Urgency (timeline mention)
Day 14: Breakup ("Should I close your file?")
```

**Fact**: 44% of salespeople give up after 1 follow-up. 48% never follow up at all.
