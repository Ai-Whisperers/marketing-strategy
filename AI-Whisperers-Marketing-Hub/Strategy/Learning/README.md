## Quick Reference

### The 5 Most Important Negotiation Principles

1. **Know Your BATNA** - Your power comes from your alternatives
2. **Never Split the Difference** - Compromise often means both lose
3. **Silence is Power** - After asking, STOP TALKING
4. **Label Emotions** - "It sounds like..." defuses defensiveness
5. **Calibrated Questions** - "How" and "What" put the ball in their court

### The 3 Sales Frameworks to Master

1. **Gap Selling** - Current State → Gap → Future State
2. **Challenger Sale** - Teach → Tailor → Take Control
3. **StoryBrand** - Customer is the Hero, You're the Guide
