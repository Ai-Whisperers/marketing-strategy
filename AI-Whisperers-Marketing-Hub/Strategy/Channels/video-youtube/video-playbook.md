## Why Video Matters

### Video ROI Statistics

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Video in email increases CTR | +200-300% |
| Landing page with video conversion boost | +80% |
| Buyers who watch video before purchase | 96% |
| B2B marketers using video | 91% |
| Video content recall vs text | 95% vs 10% |

### The Trust Factor

Video is the **highest trust-building content format** because:
- Shows real people (authenticity)
- Demonstrates expertise in action
- Harder to fake than written content
- Creates parasocial connection

> **Key Insight**: Video testimonials have the highest trust impact of any social proof format.

|----------|-----------------|-------------------|-------------------|----------|

### Platform-Specific Approach

#### LinkedIn (Primary - B2B Focus)

**Why it matters**: 80% of B2B leads come from LinkedIn

| Content Type | Engagement | Best For |
|--------------|------------|----------|
| Native video | 5x more reach than linked | Awareness |
| Talking head | High connection | Thought leadership |
| Tutorial/How-to | High save rate | Lead gen |
| Behind-the-scenes | Highest engagement | Brand building |

**Best Practices**:
- Always upload native (not YouTube links)
- Add captions (40% watch without sound)
- Post Tue-Thu, 8-10am
- 30-90 seconds optimal
- Hook in first 3 seconds

#### YouTube (Secondary - Long-form Authority)

**Why it matters**: Second largest search engine, evergreen content

| Content Type | Purpose | Length |
|--------------|---------|--------|
| Tutorials | SEO + lead gen | 8-15 min |
| Case studies | Trust building | 5-10 min |
| Shorts | Discovery + reach | 30-60 sec |

**Best Practices**:
- Optimize titles for search
- Custom thumbnails (critical)
- First 30 seconds = hook
- Include chapters/timestamps
- Clear CTA at end

## Short-Form Video Strategy

### The Short-Form Formula

```
0:00-0:03 → HOOK (grab attention)
0:03-0:25 → VALUE (deliver insight)
0:25-0:30 → CTA (what to do next)

Total: 30 seconds optimal
Max: 60 seconds for LinkedIn
```

### Best Practices for Short-Form

| Element | Recommendation | Impact |
|---------|----------------|--------|
| Hook | First 3 seconds | 65% of viewers decide to stay |
| Captions | Always include | +40% watch time |
| Format | Vertical (9:16) | Required for mobile |
| Face on screen | Personal content | +38% engagement |
| Call-to-action | End with clear CTA | +25% conversions |
| Native posting | Don't cross-post | +50% reach |

## Long-Form YouTube Strategy

### YouTube Content Strategy

**Posting frequency**: 1-2 videos per week
**Video length**: 8-12 minutes (sweet spot)
**Focus**: Searchable, evergreen content

### YouTube Content Pillars

| Content Type | SEO Potential | Conversion | Frequency |
|--------------|---------------|------------|-----------|
| Tutorials | Very High | Medium | 2x/month |
| Tool comparisons | High | High | 1x/month |
| Case studies | Medium | Very High | 1x/month |
| Industry trends | Medium | Low | 1x/month |
| Q&A/AMA | Low | Medium | 1x/month |

### YouTube SEO Checklist

- [ ] Keyword in title (front-loaded)
- [ ] Keyword in description (first 100 chars)
- [ ] 10+ relevant tags
- [ ] Custom thumbnail (text + face)
- [ ] Chapters/timestamps
- [ ] End screen with CTA
- [ ] Cards linking to related content
- [ ] Pinned comment with links/CTA

### Video Title Formulas

```
How to [Achieve Outcome] in [Timeframe]
"How to Automate Your Invoicing in 30 Minutes"

[Number] [Things] That [Outcome]
"5 Automations That Save 10+ Hours/Week"

[Tool/Method] vs [Tool/Method]: Which is Better?
"Zapier vs Make: Which Should You Choose?"

Why [Common Belief] is Wrong
"Why Most AI Projects Fail (And How to Avoid It)"

The [Adjective] Guide to [Topic]
"The Complete Guide to AI Automation for SMEs"
```

### Thumbnail Best Practices

| Element | Recommendation |
|---------|----------------|
| Face | Show face with expression |
| Text | 3-4 words max, large font |
| Colors | High contrast, brand colors |
| Size | 1280x720 minimum |
| Testing | Create 2-3 versions, test |

## Content Repurposing Framework

### One Long Video → Multiple Assets

```
One 10-minute YouTube video becomes:

├─ YouTube long-form (1x)
├─ YouTube Shorts (5-10 clips)
├─ LinkedIn videos (5-10 clips)
├─ Instagram Reels (5-10 clips)
├─ TikToks (5-10 clips)
├─ Blog post (transcript + SEO)
├─ LinkedIn carousel (key points)
├─ Quote graphics (5-10)
├─ Email newsletter (summary)
├─ Twitter/X thread (key insights)
└─ Podcast audio (if applicable)

Production Investment: 4-6 hours
Content Generated: 2-3 weeks worth
ROI Multiplier: 10-15x
```

### Repurposing Workflow

```
Week 1:
├─ Monday: Record long-form video
├─ Tuesday: Edit long-form
├─ Wednesday: Extract short clips
├─ Thursday: Create graphics/thumbnails
└─ Friday: Schedule all content

Week 2-3:
├─ Daily: Post one short-form piece
├─ Weekly: Newsletter with video embed
└─ Ongoing: Engage with comments
```

### Clip Extraction Framework

From one 10-minute video, extract:

| Clip Type | Length | Content |
|-----------|--------|---------|
| Hook clips | 15-30 sec | Opening statement |
| Tip clips | 30-60 sec | Single actionable tip |
| Story clips | 30-60 sec | Client story/example |
| Quote clips | 15-30 sec | Memorable statement |
| CTA clips | 15-30 sec | Call-to-action |
