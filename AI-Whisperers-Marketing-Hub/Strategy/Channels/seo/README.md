# SEO Strategy

> Get found when people search for what you do

---

## Why SEO?

- **Compound returns** - Content ranks for years
- **High intent** - Searchers want solutions now
- **Lower cost** - No per-click fees
- **Credibility** - Ranking = authority

---

## Our SEO Focus

### Primary Goals
1. Rank for "AI automation" related terms
2. Drive qualified traffic to lead magnets
3. Build topical authority

### Target Keywords

**High Intent (Bottom Funnel)**:
- AI automation services
- Business automation consultant
- AI implementation company
- Automation agency [location]

**Informational (Top Funnel)**:
- What is AI automation
- How to automate [task]
- AI automation examples
- Best automation tools
