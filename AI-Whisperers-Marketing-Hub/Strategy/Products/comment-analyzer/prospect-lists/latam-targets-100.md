# TOP 100 LATAM TARGET COMPANIES
## Social Media Comment Analysis Platform - Prime Targets

**Date:** November 24, 2025
**Selection Criteria:**
- Active social media presence (>100K followers)
- High comment volume (>500/month)
- Industries with strong customer service needs
- Budget for B2B SaaS ($500-5,000/month)
- Pain points: customer sentiment, churn, brand reputation

---

## SELECTION METHODOLOGY

### Scoring System (0-100 points)

**Social Media Presence (30 points):**
- Instagram followers: 1M+ = 10pts, 500K-1M = 7pts, 100K-500K = 5pts
- Facebook followers: Similar scale
- Twitter/LinkedIn engagement: High = 5pts, Medium = 3pts

**Comment Volume (25 points):**
- >5,000/month = 25pts
- 2,000-5,000/month = 20pts
- 500-2,000/month = 15pts

**Industry Fit (20 points):**
- E-commerce/Retail = 20pts (highest need)
- Telecom/Banking = 18pts (high customer service volume)
- Hospitality/Travel = 16pts
- B2C Tech = 14pts
- Other = 10pts

**Budget Capacity (15 points):**
- Large enterprise (>$500M revenue) = 15pts
- Mid-market ($50M-500M) = 12pts
- SMB ($5M-50M) = 8pts

**Pain Point Severity (10 points):**
- High negative sentiment visible = 10pts
- Moderate engagement challenges = 7pts
- Stable but could improve = 5pts
