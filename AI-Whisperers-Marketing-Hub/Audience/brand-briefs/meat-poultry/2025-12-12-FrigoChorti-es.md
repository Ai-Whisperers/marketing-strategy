## 1. PAIN POINT DIAGNOSIS (The "Why Now")
*Start here. What is bleeding?*

### PRIMARY PAIN POINT: SPANISH-ONLY BASIC SITE FOR INTERNATIONAL EXPORTER

* **Critical Gap**: FrigoChorti (Cooperativa Chortitzer) exports beef internationally and is approved for US market - but has a basic Spanish-only website with no B2B capabilities.
* **Evidence**:
    * frigochorti.com.py - Basic product showcase only
    * Spanish only despite international exports
    * No B2B portal or export documentation
    * No product specifications or technical datasheets
    * WooCommerce potential not utilized
    * No traceability demonstration despite natural/grass-fed positioning
* **Impact**:
    * **US Market Handicap**: Approved for US export but no English content
    * **Brand Story Untold**: "Grass-fed, hormone-free Chaco beef" not communicated internationally
    * **Self-Service Gap**: International buyers need specs, can't find them
    * **Sister Brand Disconnect**: Lácteos Trébol has separate site - no synergy

### SECONDARY PAIN POINT: INTEGRATED COOPERATIVE, FRAGMENTED DIGITAL

* Chortitzer owns FrigoChorti (meat) + Lácteos Trébol (dairy) + agriculture
* Each brand has separate basic website
* No unified B2B portal for international buyers wanting multiple products
