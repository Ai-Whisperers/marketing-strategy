* **Critical Gap**: Neuland exports premium beef to USA, Chile, Germany and other international markets but website is SPANISH ONLY with no multilingual support.
* **Evidence**:
    * neuland.com.py - Only Spanish language
    * No English version despite US export approval
    * No German version despite Mennonite German heritage and German market

* **Impact**:
    * **US Buyers Can't Navigate**: American importers face language barrier
    * **German Market Disconnect**: Mennonite heritage could be marketing advantage in Germany
    * **Lost Brand Story**: 100% traceability message not reaching international buyers
    * **Chile Advantage Missed**: Chile is top market, Portuguese/Spanish ok but English needed for brokers

* No certifications downloadable
