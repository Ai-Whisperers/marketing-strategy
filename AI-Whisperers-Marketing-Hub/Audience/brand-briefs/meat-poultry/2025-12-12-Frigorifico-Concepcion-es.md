## 1. PAIN POINT DIAGNOSIS (The "Why Now")
*Start here. What is bleeding?*

### PRIMARY PAIN POINT: BASIC WEBSITE FOR A 4-CONTINENT EXPORTER

* **Critical Gap**: Frigorífico Concepción exports 90%+ of production to 4 continents (Europe, Asia, Middle East, Americas) but has only a basic informational website with no B2B capabilities.
* **Evidence**:
    * Website frigorificoconcepcion.com.py is informational only
    * No B2B portal for international buyers
    * No downloadable certifications (Halal, Kosher, EU compliance)
    * No product specifications or datasheets
    * No RFQ (Request for Quote) system
    * No supply chain transparency tools
* **Impact**:
    * **Lost Efficiency**: European buyers expect self-service portals
    * **Competitive Disadvantage**: Brazilian competitors (JBS, Minerva) have sophisticated B2B platforms
    * **Compliance Risk**: EU buyers need easy access to traceability documentation
    * **Longer Sales Cycles**: Manual processes slow down international deals

### SECONDARY PAIN POINT: NO DIGITAL DIFFERENTIATION

* Despite being a premium Paraguayan brand, website doesn't showcase:
    * Farm-to-export traceability
    * Sustainability initiatives (mentioned but not documented)
    * Premium certifications in downloadable format
