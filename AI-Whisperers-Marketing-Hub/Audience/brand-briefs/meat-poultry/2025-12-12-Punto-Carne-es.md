## 1. PAIN POINT DIAGNOSIS (The "Why Now")
*Start here. What is bleeding?*

### PRIMARY PAIN POINT: BROKEN E-COMMERCE - CART SHOWS ALL CUSTOMERS' ORDERS

* **Critical Issue**: The shopping cart at puntocarne.com.py/carrito displays orders from ALL customers, not just the current user. This is a **major security flaw** and makes purchasing impossible.
* **Evidence**:
    * Direct observation of cart page showing multiple unrelated orders
    * No session isolation between users
    * Customers cannot complete purchases online
* **Impact**:
    * **Lost Revenue**: Every potential online sale is lost
    * **Security Risk**: Customer data potentially exposed
    * **Brand Damage**: Unprofessional appearance vs competitors like Frigomas
    * **Competitive Disadvantage**: Frigomas has fully functional e-commerce with "Parrillómetro" calculator

### SECONDARY PAIN POINT: BASIC WEBSITE VS PREMIUM COMPETITORS

* Frigomas (frigomas.com.py) has: Working cart, 40+ products, WhatsApp integration, meat calculator tool
* Punto Carne has: Broken cart, limited catalog, no tools
