* **Critical Gap**: Lactolanda controls 85% of Paraguay's dairy market, exports to 30+ countries, has been Exportador del Año for 11 years - but has a basic informational website with no e-commerce or B2B capabilities.
* **Evidence**:
    * lactolanda.com.py - Informational only, no e-commerce
    * No B2B portal for international buyers
    * No product specifications for export items (industrial milk powder, butter)
    * Recipe section exists but no consumer e-commerce

* **Impact**:
