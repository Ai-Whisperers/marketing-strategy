# Paid Advertising

> Accelerate growth with strategic paid campaigns

---

## When to Use Paid Ads

**Use paid ads when**:
- Organic is working and you want to scale faster
- You have a proven offer with good conversion
- You've calculated acceptable CAC
- You have budget to test and optimize

**Don't use paid ads when**:
- You haven't validated your messaging
- Your website doesn't convert
- You can't track conversions properly
- You need immediate ROI (ads need optimization time)

## Getting Started Checklist

Before running ads:
- [ ] Conversion tracking set up
- [ ] Landing page tested and converting
- [ ] Target audience defined
- [ ] Ad creative prepared
- [ ] Budget allocated
- [ ] Success metrics defined
- [ ] Testing plan created

---

## Key Metrics

| Metric | Target | Notes |
|--------|--------|-------|
| CPM (Cost per 1000 impressions) | $20-50 (LinkedIn) | Awareness |
| CPC (Cost per click) | $5-15 (LinkedIn) | Traffic |
| CTR (Click-through rate) | 0.5-1% | Ad quality |
| Conversion rate | 5-15% | Landing page |
| CPL (Cost per lead) | $50-200 | Lead gen |
| CAC (Cost to acquire customer) | < LTV/3 | Sustainability |

## Budget Recommendations

### Testing Phase (Month 1-2)
- **Total**: $1,000-2,000/month
- **LinkedIn**: 60%
- **Google**: 30%
- **Retargeting**: 10%

### Scaling Phase (Month 3+)
- Double down on best-performing channel
- Increase budget on winning campaigns
- Cut underperformers
