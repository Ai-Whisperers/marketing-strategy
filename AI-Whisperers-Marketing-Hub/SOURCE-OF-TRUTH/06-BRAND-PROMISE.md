# Source of Truth: Brand Promise

> Our core promise and what it means
> **Last Updated**: December 3, 2025

---

## The Core Promise

### Official Statement

> **"It will work. And it won't take forever."**

This is our primary brand promise. Use this exact wording in:
- Homepage
- Services page
- Proposals
- Sales conversations
- Social bios

---

## What This Promise Means

### "It will work"
- We deliver functional solutions, not half-finished projects
- What we build does what it's supposed to do
- We don't leave you with broken systems
- We stand behind our work

### "It won't take forever"
- Fast turnaround times
- No endless consultation phases
- Results in days/weeks, not months
- We respect your time

---

## How We Deliver on This Promise

### Quality Commitment
- Thorough testing before delivery
- Clear documentation
- Training so you can use what we build
- Support after launch

### Speed Commitment
- Web projects: 1-2 weeks typical
- Clear timelines upfront
- Regular communication on progress
- No surprises on delivery dates

---

## Secondary Promises

### "No Technical Skills Required"

**Definition**: Clients don't need coding or technical knowledge to use our solutions.

**How We Deliver**:
- User-friendly interfaces
- Comprehensive training
- Documentation in plain language
- Ongoing support

---

### "We Teach You to Fish"

**Definition**: Clients become self-sufficient; we don't create dependency.

**How We Deliver**:
- Hands-on training sessions
- Written documentation/SOPs
- Video walkthroughs
- Q&A support

---

## What We DON'T Promise

To stay honest, we avoid:
- Specific dollar savings claims (until we have data)
- Guaranteed ROI numbers
- "X hours saved" claims (until verified)
- Unrealistic timelines

---
