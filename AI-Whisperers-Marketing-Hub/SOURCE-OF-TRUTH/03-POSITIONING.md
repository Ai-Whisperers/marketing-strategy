## Messaging Do's and Don'ts

### Strong Headlines (USE THESE):
- "If you have to do it more than once, automate it"
- "Automate the Work You Hate"
- "Results in Days, Not Months"
- "It will work. And it won't take forever."
- "Stop Drowning in Manual Work"

### Weak Headlines (AVOID THESE):
- "Innovative AI Solutions for Enterprise" (vague)
- "Leverage Cutting-Edge Technology" (jargon)
- "Digital Transformation Partner" (overused)
- "World-Class AI Services" (empty claim)
- "Synergize Your Operations" (meaningless)

---

## Tone of Voice

### We Are:
- Clear and concise
- Results-oriented
- Confident but humble
- Direct but warm
- Expert but accessible

### We Are NOT:
- Corporate or formal
- Hype-driven or salesy
- Jargon-heavy
- Condescending
- Desperate or pushy

### Example:
Bad: "Leverage our cutting-edge AI solutions to synergize your business processes and achieve unprecedented operational efficiency."

Good: "Automate the boring stuff. Grow your business. Keep your sanity. That's what we do."
