### Key Negotiation Principles to Apply
1. **Never split the difference** - Compromise often means both lose
2. **Tactical empathy** - Understand their feelings before presenting
3. **Calibrated questions** - "How" and "What" questions (not "Why")
4. **BATNA power** - Know your walk-away point before every call
5. **Silence after the ask** - Don't fill the void with discounts
