# LinkedIn Connection Invitation Notes
## AI Whisperers - Quick Outreach (No Research Required)

**Under 200 characters. One message per segment. Personalization comes later with webscraper data.**

---

## How to Use

1. Filter LinkedIn by job title/role
2. Copy the message for that segment
3. Send to all matches
4. Personalize follow-ups once they accept

---

## By Role

### L&D / Training / HR

```
Hi, building a network of L&D folks exploring AI for workforce training. Always curious what others are seeing.
```

## Follow-Up After Acceptance

**Wait 1-2 days. Keep it simple.**

```
Thanks for connecting! Quick question - are you using any AI tools in your day-to-day yet?
```

```
Appreciate the connection. What's keeping you busy these days?
```

```
Thanks for connecting! Curious - what's your take on AI in [their field]? Hype or real value?
```

---

## Notes

- Don't overthink it - short and honest beats long and fake
- Accept rate will be lower than personalized, but volume makes up for it
- Real personalization comes in the follow-up, once webscraper is ready
- Track which segments have best accept rates and double down
