## Brand Messaging Do's and Don'ts

### Headlines That Work

**Strong Headlines**:
- "If you have to do it more than once, automate it"
- "Automate the Work You Hate"
- "It will work. And it won't take forever."
- "Stop Drowning in Manual Work"

**Weak Headlines (Avoid)**:
- "Innovative AI Solutions for Enterprise" (vague, corporate)
- "Leverage Cutting-Edge Technology" (jargon)
- "Digital Transformation Partner" (overused)
- "World-Class AI Services" (empty claim)
- "Synergize Your Operations" (meaningless)

### Email Subject Lines That Convert

**Good**:
- "Quick question about [their process]"
- "How [Similar Company] automated their workflow"
- "Your customer feedback analysis"
- "Idea for [Company]"

> **Tactic**: Offer a free sample of their online comments analyzed to show them their pain points and churn risks.

**Bad**:
- "Exciting opportunity!"
- "Let's connect!"
- "AI automation services"
- "Following up again..."

### Social Proof Phrases
*(Need stronger proof points as we gather real results)*

**Strong**:
- "Saved X hours per week"
- "From X hours to Y minutes"
- "Implemented fast"
- (Add real numbers as we gather client data)

**Weak**:
- "Loved working with them"
- "Great experience"
- "Would recommend"
- "Very professional"
