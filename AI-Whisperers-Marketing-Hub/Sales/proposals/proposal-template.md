1. Executive Summary (1 page)
2. Understanding Your Situation (1 page)

4. Investment & Timeline (1 page)
5. Why Ai-Whisperers (1 page)

## Proposal Best Practices

### Do:
- Mirror their language (use terms they used)
- Quantify everything possible
- Keep it focused (5-7 pages max)
- Make next steps crystal clear
- Include social proof
- Address concerns proactively

### Don't:
- Make it about you (make it about them)
- Use jargon they won't understand
- Include options they didn't ask for
- Bury the price (be direct)
- Send without follow-up plan
