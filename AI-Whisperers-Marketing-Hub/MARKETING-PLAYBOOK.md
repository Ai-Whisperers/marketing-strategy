# PART 4: MARKETING CHANNELS

## Priority Channels

### Priority 1: LinkedIn (Immediate)
**Why**: B2B audience, high-intent traffic
**Investment**: 2-3 hours/day
**Actions**:
- Post daily on personal profile
- Use carousel posts, polls, and video
- Engage 30 min/day on others' content
- Send 10-20 personalized connection requests/day

**Expected**: 500-1000 new connections/month, 10-20 leads/month

### Priority 2: Email Marketing (Week 1)
**Why**: Highest ROI channel, you own the audience
**Investment**: $50/mo + 5 hours/week
**Actions**:
- Lead magnet: "7 AI Automations to Save 10 Hours/Week"
- Website popup + LinkedIn bio link
- Send 1-2 emails/week

**Expected**: 500 subscribers in 3 months, 5-10% conversion to calls

### Priority 3: YouTube (Week 2)
**Why**: Searchable, evergreen, builds authority
**Investment**: 4-6 hours/week
**Content Types**:
- "AI Automation Tutorial" videos
- "How I automated [X] with AI"
- Tool comparisons
- Case study breakdowns

**Expected**: 1,000 subscribers in 6 months, 20-50 leads/month after 3 months

### Priority 4: Twitter/X (Week 2)
**Why**: Tech-savvy audience, fast engagement
**Investment**: 1 hour/day
**Strategy**: 3-5 tweets/day, quick AI tips, engage with AI/tech community

**Expected**: 2,000-5,000 followers in 6 months

```
ATTRACT → ENGAGE → CONVERT → CLOSE → RETAIN → EXPAND

10,000    1,000       100       10       8        5
visitors  leads      SQLs    customers retained  upsold
```

## Stage 1: ATTRACT (Top of Funnel)

**Goal**: Drive 10,000+ monthly visitors

**Tactics**:
- Daily LinkedIn posts
- YouTube tutorials
- SEO-optimized blog posts
- Free tools and templates
- Social media engagement

## Stage 2: ENGAGE (Middle of Funnel)

**Goal**: Capture 1,000+ email subscribers/month

**Lead Magnets**:
- AI Automation Opportunity Audit
- ROI Calculator
- Process Templates Library
- ChatGPT Prompts for Business
- Free webinars

- Weekly newsletter

## Stage 3: CONVERT (Sales Qualified Leads)

**Goal**: 100+ SQLs per month

**Conversion Points**:
- Free strategy calls
- Webinar pitches
- Email campaign offers
- Application funnels

## Stage 4: CLOSE (Sales)

**Goal**: 10+ new customers/month, 20-30% close rate

**Sales Process**:
1. Discovery call (30-45 min)
2. Custom proposal (48 hours)
3. Follow-up and close

**Pricing (Hourly/Fixed)**:

## Stage 5: RETAIN (Customer Success)

**Goal**: 90%+ completion rate, 80%+ satisfaction

**Activities**:
- Weekly check-ins during implementation
- Comprehensive training
- Complete documentation
- 90-day support included

## Stage 6: EXPAND (Upsells & Referrals)

**Goal**: 50%+ expand services, 30%+ refer

**Opportunities**:

- Ongoing optimization
