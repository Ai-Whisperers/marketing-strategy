## Key Principles

1. **Set expectations early** - Clear timeline and milestones
2. **Communicate progress** - Don't go dark during implementation
3. **Celebrate wins** - Make them feel good about the decision
4. **Ask for feedback** - Before it's too late to fix
5. **Quantify results** - Show the ROI in their language
6. **Ask for referrals** - When they're happiest

## Client Retention Strategies (From Research)

### Proactive Service Reduces Churn

| Strategy | Impact |
|----------|--------|
| Proactive customer service | -36% churn |
| Loyalty programs | +82% retention |
| Customer communities | +54% retention |
| Personalization | +77% retention |

### Retention Best Practices

**1. First Value Delivery < 7 Days**
Get the first automation working fast. Quick wins build trust.

**2. Regular Communication Cadence**
- Day 0: Welcome
- Day 1: Prep checklist
- Day 7: First milestone
- Day 14: Feedback request
- Day 30: Results review
- Day 45: Check-in
- Day 60: Expansion conversation

**3. Document Everything**
Clients who understand their systems are more confident and stay longer.

**4. Expansion > New Acquisition**
- Upsell success rate (existing clients): 60-70%
- Upsell success rate (new clients): 5-20%
- Focus on growing existing relationships

**5. Ask for Referrals at Peak Happiness**
Day 30 (results review) is optimal timing.
