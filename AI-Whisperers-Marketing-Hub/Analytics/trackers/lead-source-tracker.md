# Lead Source Tracker

> Know where your best leads come from

---

## Why Track Lead Sources?

Not all leads are equal. A lead from a referral might close 50% of the time, while a lead from cold email might close 5%.

Tracking sources helps you:
- Double down on what works
- Stop wasting time on what doesn't
- Calculate true cost per acquisition
- Predict future results

---

## Lead Source Categories

### Inbound (They Found Us)

| Source | Definition |
|--------|------------|
| **Organic Search** | Found us via Google/Bing search |
| **Direct** | Typed our URL directly |
| **Social - Organic** | From LinkedIn/Twitter posts (not ads) |
| **Referral** | Sent by existing client or contact |
| **Content** | Downloaded lead magnet, read blog |
| **Website** | Contact form, chat widget |

### Outbound (We Found Them)

| Source | Definition |
|--------|------------|
| **LinkedIn Outreach** | Cold connection requests, InMails |
| **Cold Email** | Unsolicited email outreach |
| **Cold Call** | Phone outreach |
| **Event** | Met at conference, webinar, meetup |

### Paid

| Source | Definition |
|--------|------------|
| **LinkedIn Ads** | Paid LinkedIn campaigns |
| **Google Ads** | Search or display ads |
| **Other Paid** | Facebook, Twitter, other platforms |
