## AI Automation Workflows

### Workflow 1: Blog Post ‚Üí Multi-Channel Content
1. Write blog in ChatGPT
2. Generate social posts from blog (ChatGPT)
3. Create graphics (Canva Magic Design)
4. Create short video (Runway or Canva)
5. Schedule everywhere (Buffer)
6. Extract quotes for LinkedIn carousel (ChatGPT + Canva)

**Time**: 2 hours ‚Üí Creates 1 blog + 10 social posts + 1 video + 1 carousel

---

### Workflow 2: Video ‚Üí Omnichannel Content
1. Record long-form video (Zoom/Riverside)
2. Edit in Descript (auto-remove filler words, generate transcript)
3. Create 10 short clips (OpusClip)
4. Generate blog post from transcript (ChatGPT)
5. Extract quotes for social (ChatGPT)
6. Create thumbnails (Midjourney + Canva)
7. Schedule videos (Buffer) and publish blog

**Time**: 3 hours ‚Üí Creates 1 video + 10 clips + 1 blog + 15 social posts

---

### Workflow 3: Lead Magnet Creation
1. Research topic with Perplexity
2. Generate outline in ChatGPT
3. Write content (ChatGPT + your expertise)
4. Design in Canva (Magic Design)
5. Create landing page (HubSpot)
6. Set up delivery automation (HubSpot/ActiveCampaign)
7. Create promotional content (ChatGPT)

**Time**: 4 hours ‚Üí Complete lead magnet + landing page + email sequence

---

### Workflow 4: Sales Call ‚Üí Content
1. Record call with Fireflies
2. Get transcript + summary automatically
3. Extract success story (ChatGPT)
4. Turn into case study (ChatGPT + Canva)
5. Create social posts about win (ChatGPT)
6. Add to sales deck (Canva)

**Time**: 30 minutes ‚Üí Multiple content assets + improved sales materials

## AI Tool Selection Framework

### Before Buying Any Tool, Ask:
1. **Does it save time?** Calculate hours saved vs. cost
2. **Does it improve quality?** Better results = worth paying
3. **Does it enable scale?** Can't do manually = valuable
4. **Is it actually AI or just marketing?** Many tools claim "AI" without real value
5. **Does it integrate?** Works with existing stack?

### Red Flags:
- Tool doesn't have free trial
- No clear pricing
- Makes unrealistic promises
- Requires long-term commitment upfront
- Poor reviews on G2/Capterra
