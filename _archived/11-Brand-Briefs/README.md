## BRAND BRIEF TEMPLATE

Each brand brief contains:

### 1. Company Overview
- Company name, HQ, industry
- Revenue, employees, market cap
- Stock ticker, parent company

### 2. Digital Presence
- Social media followers & engagement
- Website traffic estimates
- App store ratings (if applicable)

### 3. Pain Points Analysis
- Customer complaints (from social listening)
- Reputation challenges
- Digital transformation needs
- Training/upskilling requirements

### 4. Service Fit Assessment
- Comment Analyzer need (1-5)
- Training Courses need (1-5)
- Recommended package

### 5. Decision Makers
- Key contacts by role
- LinkedIn profiles (where available)
- Best approach strategy

### 6. Competitive Intelligence
- Main competitors
- Market position
- Differentiators

### 7. Messaging Strategy
- Value proposition tailored to company
- Pain points to address
- Proof points to cite
- Objection handling

### 8. Outreach Templates
- LinkedIn connection request
- LinkedIn InMail
- Email subject lines
- Email body templates

## HOW TO USE THESE BRIEFS

### Before Outreach:
1. Read the full brand brief
2. Note key pain points
3. Identify decision makers
4. Select appropriate messaging angle

### During Outreach:
1. Use provided templates as starting point
2. Personalize with recent company news
3. Reference specific pain points
4. Propose relevant solution

### After Response:
1. Update brief with interaction notes
2. Move to appropriate pipeline stage
3. Prepare demo tailored to their needs

## PRIORITIZATION GUIDE

### Tier 1 (Score 28-30) - IMMEDIATE ACTION
**Contact within 7 days**
- Highest potential value
- Strongest fit for both services
- Most accessible decision makers
- Expected deal size: $200K+/year

### Tier 2 (Score 24-27) - HIGH PRIORITY
**Contact within 14 days**
- Enterprise-level opportunities
- Strong fit for one or both services
- Clear digital transformation needs
- Expected deal size: $100-200K/year

### Tier 3 (Score 20-23) - MEDIUM PRIORITY
**Contact within 30 days**
- Mid-market opportunities
- Good fit for specific service
- Growing companies with emerging needs
- Expected deal size: $50-100K/year

### Tier 4 (Score 15-19) - PIPELINE
**Contact within 60 days**
- SMB or single-service opportunities
- Nurture for future growth
- Volume play
- Expected deal size: $10-50K/year
