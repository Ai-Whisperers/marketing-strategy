## Testimonial Collection Process

### How to Get Better Testimonials from Existing Clients:

**Step 1: Reach Out**
```
Email Subject: Quick favor? Would love your feedback

Hi [Name],

Hope you're enjoying the [automation/website/project] we built together!

I'm updating our website with client success stories and would love 
to feature WPG [Software/Amenities].

Could you share:
1. What challenge were you facing before we worked together?
2. What specific results have you seen? (time saved, cost reduced, etc.)
3. What was your experience working with us?

A 2-3 sentence quote would be amazing. Even better if you could record 
a short video testimonial (30-60 seconds).

As a thank you, I'll send you [incentive: discount on future work, 
free consultation hour, etc.].

Thanks!
[Your Name]
```

**Step 2: Interview (If Needed)**

Schedule 15-minute call and ask:
- What was your situation before?
- What made you decide to work with us?
- What was the implementation like?
- What specific results have you seen?
- How has it impacted your business/daily work?
- Would you recommend us? Why?

**Step 3: Draft and Get Approval**

Write the testimonial based on their words, send for approval:
- Include specific metrics they mentioned
- Keep their voice authentic
- Add their title and company
- Request photo or company logo

## Testimonial Display Variations

### Variation 1: Rotating Carousel
Cycle through testimonials every 5 seconds
- Works well with many testimonials
- Keeps page dynamic
- Good for homepage

### Variation 2: Grid Layout
Show 3-6 testimonials at once
- Visitors see social proof immediately
- Better for dedicated testimonials page
- Easy to scan

### Variation 3: Video Testimonials
Feature video testimonials prominently
- Highest trust factor
- More engaging
- Best conversion rates

### Variation 4: Logo Wall + Quotes
Company logos at top, testimonials below
- Shows breadth of clients
- Adds credibility
- Good for B2B
