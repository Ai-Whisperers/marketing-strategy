## Brand Messaging Do's and Don'ts

### Headlines That Work

**Strong Headlines**:
- "Save 10+ Hours Per Week with AI Automation"
- "Automate the Work You Hate"
- "Results in Days, Not Months"
- "AI That Works for Your Business"
- "Stop Drowning in Manual Work"

**Weak Headlines (Avoid)**:
- "Innovative AI Solutions for Enterprise" (vague, corporate)
- "Leverage Cutting-Edge Technology" (jargon)
- "Digital Transformation Partner" (overused)
- "World-Class AI Services" (empty claim)
- "Synergize Your Operations" (meaningless)

### Email Subject Lines That Convert

**Good**:
- "Quick question about [their process]"
- "How [Similar Company] saved 15 hours/week"
- "Your customer feedback analysis"
- "Idea for [Company]"

**Bad**:
- "Exciting opportunity!"
- "Let's connect!"
- "AI automation services"
- "Following up again..."

### Social Proof Phrases

**Strong**:
- "Saved 15 hours per week"
- "ROI in 60 days"
- "From 4 hours to 30 minutes"
- "Implemented in 2 weeks"

**Weak**:
- "Loved working with them"
- "Great experience"
- "Would recommend"
- "Very professional"
