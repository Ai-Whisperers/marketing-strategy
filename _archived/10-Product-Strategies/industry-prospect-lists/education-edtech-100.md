## SCORING CRITERIA (30 POINTS MAX)

### 1. Company Size (1-5 points)
- 5: $500M+ revenue, 5,000+ employees
- 4: $100M-500M revenue, 1,000-5,000 employees
- 3: $20M-100M revenue, 200-1,000 employees
- 2: $5M-20M revenue, 50-200 employees
- 1: <$5M revenue, <50 employees

### 2. Social Media Activity (1-5 points)
- 5: 1M+ followers, high engagement
- 4: 500K-1M followers
- 3: 100K-500K followers
- 2: 50K-100K followers
- 1: <50K followers

### 3. Digital Need (1-5 points)
- 5: Urgent digital transformation, visible AI adoption gaps
- 4: Active digitization, seeking competitive advantage
- 3: Moderate digital presence, growth potential
- 2: Traditional but exploring innovation
- 1: Limited digital focus

### 4. CX Volume (1-5 points)
- 5: Millions of student/customer interactions monthly
- 4: Hundreds of thousands monthly
- 3: Tens of thousands monthly
- 2: Thousands monthly
- 1: Minimal customer interaction volume

### 5. Training Budget (1-5 points)
- 5: Tech-forward, proven L&D spend, fast decisions
- 4: Corporate L&D budget, 60-90 day cycles
- 3: Budget available, requires ROI justification
- 2: Limited budget, traditional procurement
- 1: Budget constrained

### 6. Accessibility (1-5 points)
- 5: Decision-makers on LinkedIn, innovation-focused culture
- 4: Contactable with research, open to partnerships
- 3: Moderately accessible, traditional structure
- 2: Gatekeepers present, hierarchical
- 1: Very difficult to reach
