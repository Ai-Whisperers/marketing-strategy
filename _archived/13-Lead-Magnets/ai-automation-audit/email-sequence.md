## Sequence Notes

### Personalization Opportunities
- Reference specific audit results if tracked
- Customize by industry if known
- Reference company size if known

### A/B Tests to Run
- Subject lines (question vs. statement)
- Email length (shorter vs. current)
- CTA placement (early vs. late)
- Tone (casual vs. professional)

### Exit Points
- Unsubscribe (remove from sequence)
- Books call (move to sales sequence)
- Replies (personal follow-up)
- No engagement after email 5 (move to monthly newsletter)

### Metrics to Track
| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Open rate | 40%+ |
| Click rate | 10%+ |
| Reply rate | 5%+ |
| Call booking rate | 5-10% |
| Unsubscribe rate | <2% per email |
