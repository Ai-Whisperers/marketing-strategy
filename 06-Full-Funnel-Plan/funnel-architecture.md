## Stage 1: ATTRACT (Top of Funnel - TOFU) üéØ

### Goal
Drive awareness and traffic from ideal customers

### Target Metrics
- 10,000+ monthly visitors (all sources)
- 5,000+ content views
- 1,000+ social media impressions
- 500+ new profile views

### Strategies & Tactics

#### LinkedIn Attraction
- **Daily Posts**: 1-2x per day (insights, tips, stories)
- **Content Types**: Carousels, polls, videos, case studies
- **Engagement**: Comment on 10-20 posts/day in target niche
- **Keywords**: AI automation, business automation, productivity
- **Hashtags**: #AIAutomation #BusinessAutomation #Productivity

#### YouTube Attraction
- **Upload Schedule**: 2-3 videos/week
- **Video Types**:
  - Tutorials: "How to automate [X] with AI"
  - Tool reviews: "Best AI tools for [Y]"
  - Case studies: "How we saved Company X 20 hours/week"
- **SEO Focus**: Long-tail keywords, detailed descriptions
- **CTAs**: Free resources in description

#### SEO/Blog Attraction
- **Publishing**: 2-3 blog posts/week
- **Target Keywords**:
  - "how to automate [process]"
  - "[industry] automation tools"
  - "AI for [specific role]"
  - "automate [task] without code"
- **Content Types**: How-to guides, comparisons, case studies
- **Lead Magnets**: Embedded in every post

#### Twitter/X Attraction
- **Posting**: 3-5 tweets/day
- **Content Mix**:
  - Quick tips (40%)
  - Behind-the-scenes (30%)
  - Engagement/questions (20%)
  - Promotion (10%)
- **Threads**: 1-2 per week (deep dives)

#### Free Tools Attraction
- **AI ROI Calculator**
- **Automation Template Library**
- **Process Audit Tool**
- **AI Prompt Generator**
- Distribution via Product Hunt, Reddit, LinkedIn

## Stage 2: ENGAGE (Middle of Funnel - MOFU) ü§ù

### Goal
Capture contact information and nurture interest

### Target Metrics
- 1,000+ email subscribers/month
- 30%+ email open rate
- 10%+ click-through rate
- 500+ lead magnet downloads

### Lead Magnets

#### Tier 1: Low-Commitment (Awareness)
1. **Free Tools**: ROI calculator, templates
2. **Checklists**: "10-Point AI Readiness Checklist"
3. **Cheat Sheets**: "50 Best ChatGPT Prompts for Business"
4. **Short Videos**: "5-Minute AI Automation Crash Course"

#### Tier 2: Medium-Commitment (Consideration)
1. **Ebooks**: "The Complete Guide to AI Automation for [Industry]"
2. **Webinar**: "How to Automate Your [Department] in 30 Days"
3. **Case Study Pack**: "10 Real AI Automation Success Stories"
4. **Email Course**: "7-Day AI Automation Challenge"

#### Tier 3: High-Commitment (Decision)
1. **Free Audit**: "Custom AI Opportunity Assessment"
2. **Workshop**: "90-Minute AI Strategy Session"
3. **Consultation**: "Free 30-Minute Implementation Roadmap"
4. **Trial**: "Try Our Done-For-You Service Free"

### Email Nurture Sequences

#### Sequence 1: Welcome Series (5 emails)
- Day 0: Welcome + deliver lead magnet
- Day 2: Quick win tip + intro to services
- Day 4: Case study + social proof
- Day 7: Education content + resources
- Day 10: Invitation to book call or join course

#### Sequence 2: Course Consideration (7 emails)
- Day 0: Why AI automation matters now
- Day 2: What you'll learn in our course
- Day 4: Student success story
- Day 6: Common objections addressed
- Day 8: Behind the scenes of the course
- Day 10: Limited-time offer
- Day 12: Last chance (if not purchased)

#### Sequence 3: Service Consideration (6 emails)
- Day 0: How we work with clients
- Day 3: Case study with ROI breakdown
- Day 6: Our process explained
- Day 9: Meet the team
- Day 12: Free audit offer
- Day 15: Book a call

#### Sequence 4: Weekly Newsletter
- 1x per week: Mix of tips, case studies, offers
- Segment by interest (courses vs. services)
- Always include one CTA

## Stage 4: CLOSE (Sales) üí∞

### Goal
Convert sales conversations into paying customers

### Target Metrics
- 10+ new customers/month
- 20-30% close rate (from calls)
- $10K+ average deal size (for services)

### Sales Process

#### Discovery Call (30-45 min)
**Structure**:
1. Build rapport (5 min)
2. Understand current state (10 min)
3. Identify pain points (10 min)
4. Present vision of future state (10 min)
5. Introduce solution (5 min)
6. Next steps (5 min)

**Goals**:
- Qualify fit
- Understand budget
- Establish urgency
- Book follow-up or close

#### Proposal/Pitch (for $5K+ deals)
**Include**:
- Current state analysis
- Proposed solution
- Implementation timeline
- Expected outcomes/ROI
- Investment breakdown
- Case studies
- Terms and next steps

**Delivery**:
- Loom video walkthrough
- PDF document
- Live presentation (optional)
- Follow-up within 48 hours

#### Closing Techniques
- **Urgency**: "We have 2 slots this month"
- **Risk Reversal**: "60-day money-back guarantee"
- **Payment Plans**: "3-month installments available"
- **Bonuses**: "Book today, get X free"
- **Social Proof**: "Join 50+ companies already using this"

### Handling Objections

**"Too expensive"**
‚Üí ROI breakdown, payment plans, compare to cost of not solving

**"Need to think about it"**
‚Üí Clarify concerns, deadline for decision, offer smaller commitment

**"Need to talk to my partner/boss"**
‚Üí Offer to join that conversation, send materials, set follow-up

**"Not the right time"**
‚Üí Uncover real objection, discuss cost of waiting, nurture for future

**"We tried automation before and it didn't work"**
‚Üí Learn what failed, explain your different approach, offer pilot

## Stage 5: RETAIN (Customer Success) üéâ

### Goal
Ensure customer satisfaction, adoption, and success

### Target Metrics
- 80%+ customer satisfaction
- 90%+ project completion rate
- 50%+ customers requesting additional services
- <10% churn rate

### Onboarding Experience

#### Week 1: Kickoff
- Welcome email with team intro
- Kickoff call (goals, timeline, access)
- Project management tool access
- Slack/communication channel setup
- First quick win delivered

#### Week 2-4: Implementation
- Weekly check-ins
- Progress updates
- Training sessions
- Documentation delivery
- Troubleshooting support

#### Week 4+: Handoff & Optimization
- Final implementation
- Team training
- Documentation review
- Feedback survey
- Introduce account manager

### Customer Success Activities

#### Regular Touchpoints
- Weekly: Implementation phase
- Bi-weekly: First 3 months
- Monthly: Ongoing
- Quarterly: Business reviews

#### Value Delivery
- Monthly reports on ROI
- Optimization suggestions
- New automation opportunities
- Industry insights and updates
- Exclusive community access

#### Education Continuation
- Advanced training webinars
- Office hours Q&A sessions
- Resource library access
- Certification programs
- Community events

## Stage 6: EXPAND (Upsells & Referrals) üöÄ

### Goal
Grow revenue from existing customers and get referrals

### Target Metrics
- 50%+ of customers expand services
- 30%+ provide referrals
- 3x+ lifetime value vs. initial purchase

### Expansion Opportunities

#### Natural Upsells
1. **Additional Automations**: "What else can we automate?"
2. **Advanced Training**: Team-wide programs
3. **Ongoing Optimization**: Monthly retainer
4. **New Department**: Expand to other teams
5. **Integration Projects**: Connect more tools

#### Triggered Upsells
- After successful project completion
- When customer shares success story
- When customer asks about new feature
- During quarterly business review
- When customer hits growth milestone

### Referral Program

#### Incentive Structure
- **Customers**: 20% off next service or $500 credit
- **Referred Customer**: 10% off first purchase
- **Tracking**: Unique referral links + codes

#### Referral Activation
- Ask at peak happiness moments
- Email campaign to satisfied customers
- Make it easy (pre-written messages, graphics)
- Showcase referrers in newsletter
- Create affiliate tier for high-volume referrers

### Case Study Development
- Request testimonials at project completion
- Offer incentive for video testimonial
- Interview customers for deep case studies
- Ask permission to share results
- Feature them in content
